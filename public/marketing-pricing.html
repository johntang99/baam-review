Frequently asked

Questions worth answering.

What does "founding member" actually mean?
The first 50 self-serve customers and first 20 Full Service customers pay a discounted price locked forever. Even years from now, you'll still pay your founding rate as long as you stay subscribed. This is our way of rewarding early customers and gathering case studies.
What's the difference between self-serve and Full Service?
Self-serve means you log in, send review requests, reply to reviews, and run reports yourself. Full Service means we do all of that for you — you just send us your customer list each week. Same product underneath; different amount of your time required.
What happens after my free first month?
You'll be charged your founding price ($89/mo) or standard price ($99/mo) starting month 2. We email you 7 days before your card is charged, so there are no surprises. You can cancel any time before the charge.
Can I switch between self-serve and Full Service later?
Yes. Many customers start self-serve, realize they don't have time, and upgrade to Full Service after a few months. Your founding-customer pricing carries with you — you'll get the founding Full Service rate too if any spots are still available.
How does the "additional location" pricing work?
Each physical business address you manage counts as a location. If you own three acupuncture clinics, that's 3 locations. On the Growth tier, you pay $99 + 2 × $49 = $197/mo (or $89 + 2 × $39 = $167/mo founding). Each location gets its own dashboard, branding, and review profile.
Is there a contract or minimum commitment?
No. Month-to-month for everyone. Cancel anytime, no penalty. Full Service has a 30-day notice period for cancellation (so we can do a clean handoff), but no minimum term.
How is BAAM Review different from Birdeye or Podium?
Three things: (1) we're priced for local businesses, not enterprise — typically 67–82% cheaper than Birdeye, (2) we're built natively trilingual (EN / 中文 / ES) — no major US competitor does this, and (3) we offer Full Service as a real product, not an upsell. Birdeye charges $5,000–$15,000 just for setup; we waive it for founding customers.
What payment methods do you accept?
Stripe for credit cards and ACH transfers. We also accept Zelle for Full Service customers in the US who prefer it — ask us during your intro call.
