Tips for an accurate match
Verify your address with the button
Before clicking "Find my business," use the Google Maps button to confirm the address you entered points to your actual location. Typos in addresses are the #1 cause of match failures.
Match the name on Google exactly
If your Google listing says "Wei Lin Acupuncture, P.C." don't enter just "Wei Lin." Open your Google listing in a separate tab and copy the name exactly.
Website mismatch ≠ wrong business
Many Google listings have missing website fields. If we find a match by name and address but no website link, we'll flag it as a warning — you can still proceed.
