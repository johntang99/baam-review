The thank-you page is where the loop closes.

Five seconds after posting their Google review, the customer sees their own personal recommendation card with an offer attached. The framing isn't "do me a favor and share" — it's "your friends get $20 off, here's your card."

1
Re-framed CTA
Headline shifts from "While you're here" to "they get $20 off". The reviewer is now giving, not being asked.
2
Personal recommendation
Card carries the reviewer's name + quote + their Google avatar (not initials). Looks like THEM endorsing, not BAAM.
3
Offer with code
Visible discount + clearly-formatted code. Hero image optional (uploaded in admin). Expiry creates urgency.
4
Tracked share targets
FB / SMS / Copy / More. Each click writes a share_view event tied to the advocate.
