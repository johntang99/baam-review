How it works

Three steps. That's the whole product.

No campaigns, no segmentation, no four-page configuration wizard. Just a tool that does one thing extraordinarily well.

i.
Send a request

After a great visit, send your customer a text or email. One field, one button. Or hand them a QR code at the front desk.

ii.
Three quick taps

Your customer answers three friendly questions in their language — what you helped them with, how it went, one word for you.

iii.
A real review, on Google

A natural-sounding draft appears. They edit if they want, tap once, and post to Google. The whole thing takes a minute.

Built for owner-operators

A small set of well-considered features.

AI-assisted writing

Customers tell us about their experience in three taps. We turn that into a natural-sounding draft they review, edit, and post — never a fabricated review.

English, Chinese, Spanish

Your customers leave reviews in the language they actually speak. Three languages first-class from day one — not a translated UI bolt-on.

QR codes and embeds

A printable QR PDF for your front desk. A one-line script that adds a "Leave a Review" button to any website — WordPress, Squarespace, even hand-coded.

Compliance, on rails

No review gating, no incentive features that violate Google's policies, TCPA-compliant SMS templates with opt-out built in. We sweat this so you don't have to.

First-class multilingual

A draft from the same three inputs — service, rating, descriptor — rendered fluently in the language your customer chooses. None of the major US review tools do this well. We do.
